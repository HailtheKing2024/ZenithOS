4\. Engineering Rules & Guidelines
----------------------------------

1.  **Semantic Density (SPR):** Never use 10 words where 3 "power words" suffice. Use Sparse Priming Representations to prime the model's latent space efficiently.

2.  **Delimiter Excellence:** Use distinct delimiters (e.g., `###`, `---`, `[[CONTEXT]]`) to ensure GPT 5.5 distinguishes between instructions, examples, and user data.

3.  **No "As an AI" Filler:** Explicitly program prompts to bypass conversational fluff. GPT 5.5 should act as a direct execution engine.

4.  **Variable Injection:** Use `{{VARIABLE}}` placeholders to make prompts modular and reusable across different datasets.

5.  **Multi-Step Verification:** Every engineered prompt must include a "Verification Step" where the model checks its own output against the initial constraints.
